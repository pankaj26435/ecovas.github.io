High cleaning efficiency
Ever more powerful cleaning performance.
Pressure-retention system
The revolutionary pressure-retention system optimizes energy efficiency and ensures high vacuum pressure, with a surprisingly low noise level.
Floating brush 2.0
With the latest suspension design and optimized brush design, OZMO 950 can sweep up to 4 mm deep cracks.
Updated Smart Navi 3.0™ laser mapping and navigation
Ever more intelligent cleaning experience.
Efficient cleaning path
As OZMO 950 gets to know your home, it will learn the most efficient cleaning path for your unique needs. Leaving you with a flawless clean, every time.
Multi-floor mapping
Ability to scan and store multiple maps of different levels of your home. The robot will customize its clean once identifying which map it is in. You can also customize where you’d like the robot to clean in each map.
Updated Virtual Boundary™
Virtual boundary™ for not only vacuum, but also for mopping now. By using the ECOVACS Home App you can create virtual boundaries, restricting the robot from cleaning certain areas.
Custom mode
Clean your home in the way you wish. With custom mode, you can draw and designate on the map of your home and your robot will know which areas need the most attention.
OZMO™ mopping technology
Ever more excellent mopping performance.
Vacuums and mops in one go
With an innovative electronic water pump and reservoir, your robot will mop and vacuum at the same time, leaving your floor impeccably clean.
Unique intelligent controlled large water reservoir
Included in the OZMO™ , the water capacity could handle the whole house cleaning. The most important is, you can switch the water amount by mobile APP according to different needs when OZMO 950 is doing the mopping work.
5200 m(A)h battery
Ever longer working time for bigger house.
Low profile and strong mobility performance
Ever fuller coverage.
Brand new design for slimmer robot body
The low profile of only 93mm height through compact design allows OZMO 950 to enter lower spaces.
Up to 2cm high doorsills crossing ability
DEEBOT 950 can easily cross obstacles like doorsills up to 20mm, easily go through your house without hindrance for full coverage cleaning.
App Control
Clean your home from anywhere, anytime. App connectivity lets you take control and schedule a clean or monitor your current cleaning status all from the palm of your hand.
Amazon Echo
Experience convenient, hands-off controls. If you are an Amazon Echo user, you can link your smart home devices to your robot, and tell it when and how to start cleaning.
Google Home
Experience convenient, hands-off controls. If you are a Google Home user, you can link your smart home devices to your robot, and tell it when and how to start cleaning.
Automatic Charging
Never worry about charging your robot. When battery power is low, your intelligent robot will automatically return to its dock station to charge, all by itself.
Continuous Clean
When running low on charge, your robot automatically returns to its docking station. Once fully charged, it continues the clean where it left off.
Scheduled Cleaning
Never come home to a messy house again. Simply schedule a clean and let your robot take care of the rest.
Stair Safety Technology
Don’t worry about your robot falling down stairs. It will sense a drop off and will safely reverse its direction, continuing to efficiently clean your home.
Anti-Collision System
With its Anti-Collision sensors and soft cushion bumpers, your robot will avoid obstacles and protect your furniture.
Voice Reporting
Keep in the loop. Your robot will report what it is doing and give you real time updates.
OTA
By using ‘Over The Air’ technology. You can just update your APP to get the functions optimized and extended.
High Efficiency Air Filter
Breathe easy while your robot cleans. With its High Efficiency Filter, your robot will reduce airborn triggers while vacuuming.
